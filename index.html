How To Play Greedy Chops

Charlie wants some lamb chops but his greedy friend Roxy keeps stealing them! Select the left, middle or right button to choose which way to throw the lamb chop. If you pick the right direction, so that Roxy doesn't steal the chop you earn a point! If Roxy steals the chop from Charlie, Roxy wins a point. If you can get to 5 points before Roxy you win!
